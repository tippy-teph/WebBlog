Day 1 of BSCS Educational Tour

Day 1. Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center

We explored the Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center where we acquire the knowledge about the rich history of the Subic Bay Free Port Zone. With the arts exhibition displayed, we came to know how big the contriution of Aetas people in their land. With unity and helping hands, they made Subic Bay a beatiful and peaceful place.

Day 1. PAGASA Weather Station

We explored the Pagasa Weather Station where we gained insights on how technologies used to monitor and analyze weather patterns. We also discovered the different equipment used in meteorological observations and weather forecasting.

Day 1. FIRST AVIATION ACADEMY

When we visit the First Aviation Academy we gained some valuable knoweldege about the place as well as what it is for. First Aviation Academy is a school for pilots and here they study about planes and how to simulate and make one fly. In this place we also learned about their flight simulator which was made by Redbird company. We have learned that flight simulators can help students in the academy familiarize some of the basics in terms of flying a real plane or airplane.

Day 1. Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority

The visit on Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority where we gained knowledge and insights about the VTMS or the Vessel Traffic Management System. We learned that this system is a marine traffic monitoring system that use cctv and radar to keep track of vessels movements inside the area of Subic Bay. VTMS pplays an important role in securing the safety and protection of the region as well as its marine environment.
