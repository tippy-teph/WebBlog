Day 3 of BSCS Educational Tour

Day 3. Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) - Line 2

The visit we have in Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) - Line 2 Depot is one of the amazing experience we have in our educational tour for we have learned where and how they operate the trains found in Manila. We also got to experience to ride one for the very first time and visit the office they manage the system of the trains. During our visit in the LRTA- Line 2 Depot, we have learned that there are two hall of section for the maintenance of the trains. On the left side we have the heavy maintenance section and on the right side we have the light maintenance section which is a section for the check-up of the trains. The insights and information we learned was one of a kind and truly a useful one.

Day 3. National Museum of the Philippines

During our visit in the National Museum of the Philippines where we visited the Fine Arts area of the museum, different artworks are found displayed as well as the story on how that artwork came to light. This visit was an exciting one for me because I was given new knowledge about certain things like how the Spoliarium by Juan Luna was brought in the Philippines.
