Day 4 of BSCS Educational Tour

Day 4. 98Labs

98Labs is a company founded by the CEO Sir Brian Tan Seng. Their company provided End-to-End Encryption Development, Application and Infrastructure Modernization, and lastly, Development Security and Operations (DevSecOps). Their expertise are software engineering, new ventures and innovation suppport. During our visit, the ceo talked about his experience before and after he builds the company. His hardships in life drives him into making the best version of himself. With his perseverance and strategy in life, he achieve more than he deserves. With this, I learned that no matter how dark the path you have right now, or no matter how lost you are, just hang in there and fight life the way it deserves to be fight on. Just held your head high up and trust that God is with you in the process.
