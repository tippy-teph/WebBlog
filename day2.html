Day 2 of BSCS Educational Tour

Day 2. QBO Innovation Hub

When we visit the QBO Innovation Hub we learned what is the company is about and how it operates. QBO is the country's first public-private initiative for assisting startups and its mission is to create a globally competitive startup in the Philippines. With them, we learned that our ideas mmatter and they are the one who can help us achieve that idea come to life.

Day 2. Packetworx Iot Technology Hub

Packetworx is the first and only public LoRaWan wireless connectivity provider for the Internet of Things (IoT) in the Philippines. When we visit their company we learned a lot especially about the devices their created which made us realized the amazing things technology can do. Their company build solutions to help manage business operations, monitor their building equipment and other assests remotely, efficiently at a low maintenance cost using IoT technology.
