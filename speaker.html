The Speakers & Tourist Guide

Meet the exceptional and professional individuals who shared their knowledge and experience to us in terms of understanding the the various industrties that may help us shape our future. With the wisdom they shared, it ignited a passion between individuals who wanted to excel in their expertise and be a better person. Our respectable and esteemed speakers and tourist guide are:





Ms.Karen Montealgre

SBMA Tourist Guide

During our time in Subic Bay Zambales, we were guided by the energetic with sunshine energy Miss Karen. And all throughout our journey, she fills us with different ideas and knowledge about the beauty of Subic Bay. With her guide, we've learned the history of how Subic Bay came up to be as what it is today. With Miss Karen as our guide, we have an amazing and unforgetable Subic Bay experience.

Mr. Arnel G. Gonzales

PAGASA Weather Station Speaker

The presentation of Mr. Gonzales provided us with the rich information on how to monitor the weather and how a single mistake on it can ruin a whole operation. Mr. Gonzales provided us with knowledge about the important role of PAGASA in weather forecasting as well as how to monitor wind and heat. We gained valuable insights into the tools and system PAGASA used to gather data and analyze weather pattern reports.

Sir Dave

FAA Aviation Academy Speaker

Sir Dave presents us the idea on how a flight simulator work. With his guide and information given to us, we have learned that flight simulators can help students in the academy familiarize some of the basics in terms of flying a real plane or airplane.

Engr. Jerome D. Saddi

Port Operation Division-SD Speaker

Engineer Jerome Saddi presents us the VTMS or the Vessel Traffic Management System. In his presentation, we learned that this system is a marine traffic monitoring system that uses cctv and radar to keep track of vessels movements inside the area of Subic Bay. His explanations gaves us a deeper understanding on how important VTMS in securing the safety and protection of the region as well as its marine environment.
